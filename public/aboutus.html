What We Offer

At Aptos Asset Management, we offer a comprehensive solution for securely storing, sending, and receiving Aptos within the Metamask Snap, ensuring a seamless asset management experience. Our faucet accelerates development by providing instant access to test and dev Aptos for debugging and testing. With accurate gas estimation, users can optimize transaction costs, while our transaction log enhances transparency. Real-time APT to USD conversion keeps users updated on their holdings' value, and on-chain account creation simplifies management. Leveraging our advanced Bidirectional LSTM model, users gain insights into future price movements.
